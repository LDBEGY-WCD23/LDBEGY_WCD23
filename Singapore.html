SINGAPORE TIPS

Good to know :

  • Singapore is a very safe and well-organized city.
  • Arrival :

    Singapore still follows all passengers entering the country to limit the entry of passengers with signs of Covid. It is mandatory to fill in the Singapore Arrival Card (electronic health declaration) before arrival to receive confirmation of entry:

    https://www.ica.gov.sg/enter-transit-depart/entering-singapore/sg-arrival-card
  • Language :

    English is one of the official languages in Singapore. Everyone is fluent in English, so it is very easy to communicate.

  • Getting around :
    Metro – MRT:

    The MRT is very safe, clean, spacious, air-conditioned and esay to use. No need for purchasing tickets. Simply pay with Apply Pay or a credit card – flash your phone/card at the entry and exit of the metro station. Suntec City is easily accessible by three MRT stations (see MRT map) - Esplanade or Promenade via the Circle Line, and City Hall via the East West Line. Most MRT stations are connected to malls underground and have several exits that are well marked. But navigating the underground hallways can take a bit of time, so plan enough time for commuting.

    MRT MAP
    Taxi – GRAB:

    Uber is not available in Singapore. The local version of Uber in South Asia is GRAB. We recommend downloading the app for easy taxi access around the city. Taxi – GRAB

    City Mapper:

    is a great app to help you navigate around the city by foot and public transport. City Mapper

  • Paying in restaurants, shops, etc.. :

    you rarely need cash in Singapore, everything can be paid by Apple Pay or credit card. 1 SGD = 0,68€

  • Weather :

    Singapore is a tropical country; it is hot and humid every day! We recommend always carrying a small umbrella, sunglasses and sun protection as the weather can quickly change from sun to rain and back again!

    All interiors are strongly air-conditioned. Keep a cover-up with you as it will get cold in the WCD meeting rooms, restaurants, etc.
