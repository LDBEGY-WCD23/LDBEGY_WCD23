CONCORDE HOTEL, SINGAPORE
Get Direction

Set in a modern building that also houses a mall, this sophisticated hotel on Orchard Road is surrounded by shops. It's 3 km from lively Chinatown and a 6-minute walk from Dhoby Ghaut MRT station. Relaxed rooms come with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and DVD players, as well as minibars. Family rooms offer bunk beds, while upgraded rooms add balconies. Suites feature living rooms, and some have whirlpool baths. Room service is available 24/7. Dining options include a refined restaurant and Japanese eatery, plus a cocktail lounge. There's also a bar next to an outdoor pool with a kids' section, and a gym.
